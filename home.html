The Foolery's purpose is simple: We aspire to spread joy, laughter, and fun into the everyday lives of people through geeky gifts, funny gags, and cool novelty items.

The Foolery aims to sell assorted products, but mainly novelty items sourced abroad. Our plan is to procure hard-to-find, but functional and marketable novelty products at an affordable price. We plan to sell our items on social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram, through the business' own website, and through various bazaars to capture the shopper's market.
