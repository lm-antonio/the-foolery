How to Order and Other Frequently Asked Questions:

Hey there, Fooler! Welcome, and thanks for checking out The Foolery! 🎟🎟🎟 Here, you’ll find all questions related to ordering and receiving products from us. We streamlined and simplified the process as much as possible, to make it easy for you, but if there’s anything you need clarifications with, please don’t hesitate to send us a message. Thanks, and have fun! 🤹

    ► Just send us a private message with the following details:
  • 👽 Complete Name
  • 📞 Contact Number
  • 🏠 Complete Address
  • ❤️ Items and Quantity
  • 💵 Preferred Mode of Payment (BDO/BPI/Meet-Up)
    ► BPI and BDO 💳
  • We’ll be sending you the account details as soon as you make the order.
  • You can pay via online transfer or bank deposit, whichever you prefer.
  • Please take a picture of the deposit slip or a screenshot of the online transfer confirmation as proof of payment, and send them to us so we can send your item right away.
    ► Meet-Up 🤝
  • We encourage meet-ups because we understand that buying online can be sketchy at times, especially for first-time buyers; we also acknowledge that some people want to see and feel the product first before buying it.
  • However, we can only do meet-ups at Bonifacio High Street and/or SM Marikina at certain times of the day because of work and other scheduling conflicts.
  • If you want to push through with the meet-up, we’ll let you know the earliest date and time that we’re available.
    ► We use Xend as our official courier.
  • Your packages can be tracked, and we’ll be giving you the tracking number immediately after we receive it.
    ► Shipping fees vary per item, but it usually ranges from ₱69 - ₱120 for Metro Manila deliveries and ₱109 - ₱199 for provincial deliveries.
  • Please refer to the items’ product details for their corresponding shipping fees.
    ► Confirmation of Orders ✅
  • It will usually take us an hour or two to reply to your private message, but once we reply, that means that we’ve already confirmed your order.
  • Once we’ve confirmed your order, we’ll reserve the item for you, provided you settle the payment within two business days.
  • Failure to settle the payment within two business days means we’ll have to let go of your reservation. :-(
    ► Shipping of Orders 🚚🚚🚚
  • As soon as we receive the proof of payment, we’ll process the shipping of your item right away!
  • If you send us the proof of payment BEFORE 1 PM, then we’ll be able to give your item to the courier on that same day. ⏳
  • If you send us the proof of payment AFTER 1 PM, then your item will be given to the courier the next day. ⌛
  • We ship from Mondays to Fridays only.
  • For proofs of payment received on the weekend, we’ll process the shipment of your item first thing the following Monday.
    ► Arrival of Orders 📬
  • Within Metro Manila: 1-2 days after receiving the proof of payment
  • Outside Metro Manila: 3-4 days after receiving the proof of payment
    ► For as long as we haven’t given the item to the courier yet, then yes, you can cancel your order... but that would be very sad. 💔
    ► All our products undergo strict quality inspections before we ship them out to you, but in the highly unlikely and unfortunate event that you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us so we can resolve the problem immediately. 🔍💯
